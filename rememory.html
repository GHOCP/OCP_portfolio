Re-Memory

Product Designed for People with Dementia

This is a group project for the design challenge from the “User Experience Design” course, collaborating with a local nursing house for seniors (KBO). We delved into creating “Warm Technology”, which pivots around making the lives of people suffering from dementia better. Initiated from the problem statement based on previous research, a prototype of the proposed game was created (2022 Fall).

  • Changpei Ou(me): Project manager | Mind maps | Brainstorming director | Prototyping (physical and 3D models)
  • Milo Simons: Game-play design | Scenario | COCD analysis
  • Jasmijn de Joode: Mind maps | Affinity diagram | Testing
  • Bram Hoesdemakers: Observation | Interview
  • Xuhui Wang: Prototyping

* Other activities, such as brainstorming, literature collecting, and qualitative data analysis were executed collectively.
