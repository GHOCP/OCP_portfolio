"Hobb!": An AI-driven Character-based Activity App

This is an AI-driven group project aiming at recommending activities for university students who seek a richer social life, mainly based on their personalities analyzed by using artificial intelligence techniques. The finalized trained AI model and our aim were reflected by a mobile App. I mainly took roles in product brainstorming and user interface design.

  • Veerle Uhl: Data mining | Model building
  • Thijmen Poell: Data mining | Service blueprint
